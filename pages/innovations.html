Key Innovations

Major Developments in Computing

Microprocessor (1971)

The invention of the microprocessor by Intel revolutionized computing, making personal computers possible.

Graphical User Interface (1981)

GUI made computers accessible to everyone, moving away from text-only interfaces.

Internet (1983)

The birth of the Internet connected the world and transformed communication, business, and education.

Artificial Intelligence (1956-Present)

AI has evolved from simple rule-based systems to modern machine learning and deep learning, impacting every industry.

Did You Know? The first microprocessor, Intel 4004, could only process 4 bits at a time!
