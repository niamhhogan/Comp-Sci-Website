Emerging Trends: Flashcards

Tip: Click or tap a card to flip it.

Miniaturisation & Mobile

Smaller, more powerful devices. Smartphones and tablets combine communication, processing, and storage. Portable and always accessible.

Cloud Computing

Software, storage, and processing delivered over the internet. Access anywhere; enables collaboration, scalability, flexibility.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Everyday objects connected to the internet (smart devices, wearables). Collect/share data; smart environments; privacy/security issues.

AI & Machine Learning

AI performs tasks needing human intelligence. ML learns from data. Used in speech recognition, search, autonomous vehicles, assistants.

AR & VR

VR creates immersive digital environments; AR overlays digital content on the real world. Used in education, healthcare, engineering, entertainment.

Cybersecurity

Protects systems and data from attacks (viruses, phishing, hacking). Involves encryption, authentication, monitoring.

Green Computing

Reduce environmental impact: energy-efficient hardware, renewable energy, recycling, sustainable design. A growing priority.

Quantum Computing

Uses qubits for complex calculations beyond classical computers. Potential in cryptography, medicine, materials; still experimental.
